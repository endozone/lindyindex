# lindyindex
The Lindy Index 
Based on the 'Lindy effect', which posits that the future life expectancy of something - say, a habit, technology, or business - is proportional to its current age. Now, the term is synonymous with endurance. When applied to stocks, 39 names stand out across a variety of sectors ranging from banking to medicine. Explore the companies in question, see how it perfoms against the S&P 500, and compare up to three different companies in this interactive website.
